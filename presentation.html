ABOUT SUPER FIGHT

SuperFight is an adrenaline-fueled retro arcade boxing game that pits pixel-art fighters against each other in high-stakes combat. Experience deep mechanics including slips, pulls, dodges, and a wide array of devastating punches.

Go head-to-head with adaptive AI or challenge your friends in local 2-Player Versus mode! Build your Super meter by landing clean shots and unleash thunderous finishing moves to secure the knockout.
